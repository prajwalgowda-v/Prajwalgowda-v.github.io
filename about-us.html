Our Story

From a small startup to India's leading solar energy provider

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Naptico Green Energy Solars began with a simple vision: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to every Indian. Our founders, a team of passionate engineers and environmentalists, saw the immense potential of solar energy to transform India's energy landscape.

Starting with just a small office in Bangalore and a handful of dedicated employees, we focused on providing high-quality solar solutions to residential customers. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction quickly earned us a reputation as a reliable solar provider.

Today, we've grown into a national leader in the solar industry with over 500 employees, offices in 12 major cities, and a track record of 50,000+ successful installations. But our core mission remains the same: to accelerate India's transition to clean energy.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To accelerate India's transition to sustainable energy by providing innovative, reliable, and affordable solar solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities to harness the power of the sun.

Vision: To be the most trusted and innovative solar energy company in India, leading the country toward a future powered by 100% renewable energy and setting new standards for excellence in the industry.

We envision a future where every home and business in India is powered by clean, renewable energy, reducing carbon emissions and creating a healthier planet for future generations.
