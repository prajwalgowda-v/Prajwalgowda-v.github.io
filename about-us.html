About Naptico Green Energy Solars

Pioneering sustainable energy solutions for a brighter tomorrow. With over a decade of experience, we've become India's most trusted solar energy provider.

Our Story

From a small startup to a leading solar energy provider

Founded in 2010, Naptico Green Energy Solars began with a simple vision: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to every home and business in India. What started as a small team of passionate environmentalists has grown into a nationwide movement, with over 50,000 successful installations and 200+ MW of clean energy generated.

Our journey has been marked by innovation, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability. We've faced challenges, celebrated milestones, and continuously evolved to meet the growing energy needs of our country while reducing our collective carbon footprint.

Today, Naptico Green Energy Solars stands as a testament to what's possible when vision meets determination. We're not just installing solar panels; we're powering a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come.
