Our Story

From a humble beginning to becoming India's most trusted solar energy provider

2010

Humble Beginnings

Founded with a vision to make solar energy accessible to every Indian household, we started with a small team of 5 passionate engineers.

2015

Expansion Phase

Expanded operations to 5 major cities and completed our first 1000 installations, establishing ourselves as a reliable solar solutions provider.

2018

Innovation Hub

Launched our R&D center focused on developing more efficient solar panels and energy storage solutions, filing 12 patents.

2020

National Recognition

Awarded "Best Solar Company in India" by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy for our contribution to clean energy adoption.

2023

Milestone Achievement

Reached 50,000+ installations and 200+ MW of clean energy generated, becoming one of India's largest solar energy providers.

Mission & Vision

Our commitment to a sustainable future

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy by providing high-quality, affordable solar solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities to reduce their carbon footprint and achieve energy independence.

We are committed to innovation, excellence, and customer satisfaction in every solar installation we undertake, ensuring maximum efficiency and long-term reliability.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in India's renewable energy revolution, powering millions of homes and businesses with clean, sustainable solar energy by 2030.

We envision a future where solar energy is the primary source of power for all, contributing significantly to global efforts in combating climate change and creating a greener planet for future generations.
