Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save with solar panels?

Most homeowners save between 70-90% on their electricity bills after installing solar panels. The exact savings depend on your energy consumption, the size of your solar system, and local electricity rates. Our savings calculator can provide a personalized estimate for your specific situation.

What is the lifespan of solar panels?

Solar panels typically have a lifespan of 25-30 years. Most manufacturers offer a performance warranty that guarantees at least 80% production after 25 years. With proper maintenance, your solar system can continue generating clean energy for decades.

Are there government subsidies for solar installation?

Yes, the Indian government offers various subsidies and incentives for solar installation. These include capital subsidies, tax benefits, and accelerated depreciation. The exact benefits depend on your location and system size. Our team can help you navigate the available subsidies and maximize your savings.

How long does the installation process take?

The installation process typically takes 3-7 days from the site survey to completion. This includes design, permitting, installation, and inspection. Larger commercial installations may take longer. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily activities.

What maintenance is required for solar panels?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dust and debris is typically sufficient. We recommend professional inspection once a year to ensure optimal performance. Our maintenance plans include cleaning, inspection, and any necessary repairs to keep your system running efficiently.
